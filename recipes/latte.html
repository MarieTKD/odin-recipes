Caramel Latte

Description:

This caramel latte is delicious and so easy to make, you will never go buy one at a cafe again.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar-free caramel topping
  • 1 tablespoon caramel sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup coffee

Steps:

  1. Combine milk and brown sugar in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Heat until frothy and close to simmering, about 3 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to low; whisk in caramel sauce and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix in coffee.
  5. Enjoy!
